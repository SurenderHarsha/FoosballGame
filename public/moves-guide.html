🌀 Ball Spin Physics
The ball has a spin property that affects its trajectory via the Magnus effect. When the ball spins, it curves perpendicular to its direction of travel. Spin is applied by the Spinneroni move and slightly by normal kicks if the rod is moving. Spin decays over time and partially reverses on wall bounces. A spinning ball shows a rotating cross pattern with a purple tint — the faster the spin, the more visible the indicator.

Goalkeeper (GK)

📸
Selfie Shot F F GK

Score on yourself. A self-goal for laughs, trolling, or honoring a gentleman's agreement.

How to Execute
Select GK rod (Q). With the ball near your goalkeeper, double-tap F within 500ms to confirm. The ball fires into your own goal.
Skill Required
Courage (or a sense of humor). No mechanical skill needed — just the will to do it.

Defender (DEF)

🍝
Spaghetti Shot F DEF

Maximum power straight shot from defense toward the enemy goal. The ball fires like a rocket.

How to Execute
Select DEF rod (W). Position the ball centered on one of your defenders. Press F. The shot fires straight at max speed.
Skill Required
Ball centering. Accuracy depends on how perfectly the ball is aligned with your player's center. Off-center = random deviation. Perfect center = laser-straight shot.
🍕
Pepperoni G DEF

Random wall-bounce lucky shot. The ball fires at a random angle, bounces off a wall, and may find its way to the goal.

How to Execute
Select DEF rod (W). Ball near your defender. Press G. The ball fires at a random wall angle with high power.
Skill Required
None — it's a gamble! The angle is randomized. Sometimes genius, sometimes terrible. Use when you're feeling lucky.
🥔
Cornetto / Double Cornetto H DEF

Cornetto: Ball sticks to your player. Move the rod to control it, then click to fire a max-power straight shot.
Double Cornetto: When the ball is between two defenders, it auto-dribbles between them first, then you click to shoot.

How to Execute
Select DEF rod (W). Press H with ball near defender. The ball sticks to the nearest man (or dribbles between two). Move the rod to position, then click to fire. Best timing window is around 1 second into the stick phase.
Skill Required
Rod control + timing. Keep smooth, controlled movements while the ball is stuck. Click at the optimal timing window for maximum shot accuracy. Too early or too late = reduced accuracy.
🪐
Yoyo Shot J DEF

Fires the ball at the wall beside the enemy goal. The ball bounces off the back wall and returns to your defender area — like a yoyo.

How to Execute
Select DEF rod (W). Ball near defender. Press J. The ball fires toward the enemy back wall, aiming beside the goal. It bounces and returns to your side.
Skill Required
Positional awareness. The ball automatically aims beside the goal to avoid scoring. Use it as a playful reset or to confuse opponents. Follow up with another shot when it returns.

Midfield (MID)

🐠
Dory Shot F MID

Powerful straight shot from midfield. Accuracy is inversely proportional to your mouse movement — the stiller you are, the straighter it goes.

How to Execute
Select MID rod (E). Ball near midfield. Stop moving your mouse completely, then press F. The shot fires at max power.
Skill Required
Mouse stillness. Any mouse movement at the moment of pressing F reduces accuracy. Master players can position the ball, freeze, and fire with perfect precision. It's a test of composure under pressure.
🌪
The Eliza G MID

Rapidly slides the rod sideways. The ball slams into the wall at maximum speed and bounces in a random direction. Pure chaos.

How to Execute
Select MID rod (E). Ball near midfield. Press G. The rod oscillates rapidly for a brief moment. If the ball contacts a player during the oscillation, it flies toward the nearest wall.
Skill Required
Embrace the chaos. No precision needed — the direction is intentionally unpredictable. Best used to disrupt a stalemate or when you're behind and need a miracle.
↗
Diagonal Shot H MID / ATK

Shoots diagonally from the edge of the rod toward the opposite corner of the enemy goal. Only activates when the ball is at the top or bottom extremity of the rod.

How to Execute
Select MID (E) or ATK (R) rod. Move the rod so the ball is at the very top or bottom of the rod's range. Press H (MID) or G (ATK). The ball fires diagonally toward the opposite goal corner.
Skill Required
Ball positioning. The ball MUST be at the extremity of the rod — the move fails otherwise. Position the ball at the top to shoot toward the bottom goal corner, or vice versa. Requires spatial awareness of the table.

Attacker (ATK)

■
PP Square F ATK

Ball auto-dribbles between your attackers. Click at the perfect moment to fire a power shot at the goal.

How to Execute
Select ATK rod (R). Ball near attackers. Press F to start the dribble. The ball bounces between your attackers automatically. Click during the dribble to shoot. "CLICK TO SHOOT!" appears on screen.
Skill Required
Timing. The shot quality depends on WHEN you click. Best timing window is around 1 second into the dribble (peaks at ~60 frames). Too early, too late, or after 3 seconds = weak/inaccurate shot. Auto-cancels after 3 seconds.

All Rods (Universal)

🌀
Spinneroni V ALL

The ultimate spin shot. Applies maximum spin to the ball, causing it to curve dramatically via the Magnus effect. The hardest move in the game.

How to Execute
1. Ball must be near your selected rod.
2. Press V to enter charge mode. A spin charge bar appears.
3. Rapidly move mouse left/right to build spin charge. The faster and more you move, the more spin.
4. Click to fire the shot with accumulated spin.
5. Auto-fires after 2 seconds if you don't click.
Skill Required
Maximum horizontal mouse speed + timing. You must generate rapid horizontal mouse movement during the 2-second charge window while NOT losing control of your rod position. The ball curves proportionally to spin charge. Full charge = dramatic curve that can bend around defenders. This is the highest skill-ceiling move in the game.
👀
Fake Shot B ALL

Your rod performs a kick animation, but intentionally misses the ball. The visual deception can trick your opponent into reacting prematurely.

How to Execute
Any rod selected. Press B. The rod swings as if kicking, but the foot doesn't extend far enough to contact the ball. Works even without ball proximity.
Skill Required
Deception. No mechanical skill — it's a mind game. Use before a real shot to bait your opponent into moving their defender. Timing the fake before the real attack is key.
⚡
Dribble Shot B B ALL

Double-tap B: first tap fakes, second fires a real max-power shot. A quick deception-into-attack combo.

How to Execute
Any rod selected, ball nearby. Tap B once (fake kick plays), then tap B again within 500ms. The second tap triggers a real powerful kick. The rapid fake-to-real catches opponents off guard.
Skill Required
Fast double-tap timing. The two taps must be within 500ms. Too slow = two separate fake shots. The deceptive rhythm is the skill — your opponent sees the fake and relaxes, then the real shot arrives.
